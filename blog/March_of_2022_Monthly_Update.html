March of 2022 Monthly Update

March was a special month for the ESP Core and its team. In this month, we completed tons of work that has better our services for all of you. In this month, we first began the redesigning process of our website, along with the changes to our services. We have modernized it all, from the little text blocks to the process itself thanks to the growth our team has had this month. This month alone, we have set a new record for hours contributed to the services health than any other month. We have introduced our VPN detection system, DDoS Prevention System, & many more amazing community suggested features.

Our team is happy to announce that in the upcoming month, April, we will be releasing a large variety of different packages you and your friends can choose from. Each package will come with its very own perks, along with the ability to buy a giftable key for friends and family. This feature is still in testing and may take some time before it's 100% ready for your guys' usage, but we expect it to be ready by the end of April at the latest.

In April, we will also be introducing a new web-page where you guys, the community can directly submit suggestions to our team. This page will be reviewed by our team by the hour, so expect to see a ton of new features to come around.

Once again, we thank you guys for another great month for not just us, but the websites we secure. See you in April!
