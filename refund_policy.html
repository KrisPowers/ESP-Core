Refund Policy

If you or a friend is looking to refund your key, there are specific things that may be met for you to do so. But first, let it be known that the team is looking to provide what's best for you and other customers. If you are not satisfied, we are happy to give you a refund as long as it's within the week of you making your purchase. We would like that you leave a reply with what made you not like our services when refunding.

When attempting to make a refund request form, you need to confirm that you meet the following requirements. Your purchase must have been within the last week, your key must not have been used for any ill intentions over that time frame, & it must be linked to a website or software. If any of these requirements are not met, your refund request will be denied by our team.
